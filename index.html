The Rules of War

Two Ways to Die.

Hollow Throne isn't just combat. Every resource has a purpose — and a price you'll feel.

I

Dual Loss Conditions

Reduce your opponent's Life Points to 0 through combat — or drain their Sanity Points to 0 through spell costs and direct pressure. Both tracks are always live. Neither is safe to ignore.

II

Deck Construction

Cut your hero's 19-card pool to 15 before each match. Then secretly draft the shared Marketplace with your opponent — three picks each, revealed simultaneously, filled randomly. No two games are identical.

III

Reactive Windows

Spells don't sleep. Two windows open on your opponent's turn — after summon, and after attackers are declared. Cast into them, but know the cost: any reactive spell is permanently removed from the game.

IV

Everything Has a Cost

End-of-turn discards cost 1 LP or SP. Exhaust abilities tap creatures. Marketplace buys drain resources. Every powerful spell burns forever. The throne exacts its toll on every decision you make.
